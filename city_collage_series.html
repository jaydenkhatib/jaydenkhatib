City Collage Series

This is a series of collages I made about cities that have been important in my life for ARTF 1200.

This first collage is about Boston. I chose
to focus mostly on bands and venues from in and
around Boston since leaving my hometown music scene
was my biggest hangup about moving up here.

This collage is about Beirut. I chose Beirut because
it was the first place I ever visited outside of
North America (and it has really nice architecture).

This collage is about Asheville. This is my favorite
city in the U.S.

This collage is about Miami. Half of my family is from
Miami or West Palm Beach.

This collage is about trains. These are transit lines
or stops from a bunch of different cities I've been to.

This collage is about Portland. This is my second
favorite city in the U.S.
