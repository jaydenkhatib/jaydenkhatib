About Me

I'm a sophomore journalism and interaction design student at Northeastern University. Currently, I'm an intern at Education Week's Market Brief and managing editor of the Huntington News, our student paper. Previously, I've served as deputy city editor and data editor for the paper. I'm originally from Atlanta, Georgia.
